Favorites

Out of all of Jeffery Deaver’s works, The Bone Collector stands out as my favorite. The combination of a brilliant but physically limited detective, Lincoln Rhyme, and his sharp, determined partner Amelia Sachs creates a dynamic and unforgettable investigative team. The novel’s suspense, forensic detail, and constant twists kept me hooked from start to finish. It’s a perfect example of Deaver’s ability to blend intelligence, tension, and emotion into a truly compelling crime thriller.
